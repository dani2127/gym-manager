PowerFit Gym Rules & Regulations

General Rules

  • All members must check in at the front desk upon arrival
  • Valid membership card or QR code is required for entry
  • Proper athletic attire and clean workout shoes are mandatory
  • Personal belongings should be stored in lockers
  • The gym is not responsible for lost or stolen items

Equipment Usage

  • Return all equipment to its proper place after use
  • Wipe down equipment with provided sanitizing wipes
  • Do not drop weights - use proper form at all times
  • Report any damaged or malfunctioning equipment immediately
  • Ask for assistance if unsure how to use equipment

Etiquette

  • Be respectful of other members and staff
  • Keep noise to a minimum - no grunting or loud conversations
  • Cell phones should be on silent mode
  • No photography or video without management approval
  • Keep food and drinks in designated areas only

Safety

  • Stay hydrated - water stations are available throughout the gym
  • Do not exercise if you feel unwell or dizzy
  • Emergency exits must be kept clear at all times
  • Report any accidents or injuries to staff immediately
  • First aid kit is located at the front desk

Membership

  • Membership is non-transferable
  • Notify staff of any changes to personal information
  • Membership fees must be paid on time
  • Violation of rules may result in membership suspension

By joining PowerFit Gym, you agree to abide by these rules. Management reserves the right to modify these rules at any time.
